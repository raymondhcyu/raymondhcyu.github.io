ABOUT


As a mechatronics engineering student with a strong interest in aviation and new technologies, I am taking a gap year from my studies to work at Airbus Commercial Aircraft, where I am striving to develop innovative systems to incorporate into the next generation of aircraft. While I have over a year's worth of experience working in industrial manufacturing, smart glass R&D, as well as experience leading my university UAV team to international competitions, I recognize that my true career passion is towards the latter: unmmaned aircraft systems. Applications and technologies are continuously being developed, and it is certain that pilotless aircraft will occupy the future skies. My goal is to contribute to the proliferation of drones into society - from urban air mobility to package delivery.

If you think I can help your organization achieve these goals, please let me know!
